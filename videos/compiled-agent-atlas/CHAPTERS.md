## Stitching rules

- Act titles are integrated 3-second moves inside the first shot of each act; there are no standalone title-card pauses.
- One central agent core persists across all acts: a pulse rail becomes a modular surface, then a nested boundary system.
- The three act cuts begin on the title move and end on a 750 ms hold of their practical conclusion.
- The master preserves continuous MIDI phase. Act cuts begin each score variation at bar one.
- Captions restart their local clock at zero in standalone renders.
